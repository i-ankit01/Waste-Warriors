Welcome to Our Waste Management System

Our Smart Waste Management System is designed to streamline and enhance your waste disposal experience. Here's how our system works:

Start Your Journey

Get started with our waste management system by signing up on our main page. Join us in making waste management efficient and eco-friendly!

Schedule Waste Pickups

Easily schedule pickups for your waste through our intuitive scheduling system. View and manage your scheduled pickups in the Scheduled Pickup section of our platform.

Track Your Pickup

Monitor the status of your waste pickups in real-time. Our tracking feature provides updates on the location and estimated arrival time of your waste collector, ensuring a seamless experience.

Earn Reward Points

Receive reward points based on the type and amount of waste you donate. Points can be accumulated and used for various purposes including bill payments, online shopping, and more.

Redeem Your Points

Use your earned points to redeem rewards such as discounts on bills, online shopping credits, or other benefits. The Rewards section of our system provides details on how to redeem your points.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need assistance, our customer support team is here to help. Visit the Contact Us page to get in touch with us.
