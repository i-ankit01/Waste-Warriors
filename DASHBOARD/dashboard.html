Why Recycling Matters

Environmental Impact

By recycling, you help:

  • Reduce Greenhouse Gas Emissions: Recycling reduces the need for new raw materials, which decreases emissions from manufacturing processes.
  • Save Energy: Recycling uses less energy compared to producing products from virgin materials, leading to lower energy consumption.
  • Conserve Natural Resources: Recycling helps conserve valuable natural resources such as metals, minerals, and trees.
